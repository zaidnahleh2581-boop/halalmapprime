How the app will work

Halal Map Prime is designed to stay simple, fast and focused on halal locations. Here’s the basic flow when you open the app:

  1. Open the app and land on the map with your approximate location (with your permission).
  2. Choose a category: halal restaurants, mosques, markets or butchers.
  3. Tap a pin to see essential details: name, address, phone, basic halal/owner notes.
  4. Use search to jump to a different area or look for a specific place by name or cuisine.
  5. In Ramadan mode (coming soon), quickly see iftar-friendly places and useful daily tools.

Our goal is not to overwhelm you with social features. We want a clean utility app that simply answers one question: “Where is the nearest halal place I can trust?”

Questions

Frequently asked questions

Is the app already available to download?
Not yet. We are finalizing the first release and preparing the App Store submission. In the meantime, you can follow us on Instagram and Facebook or send us an email to be notified as soon as it goes live.
Which areas does Halal Map Prime cover?
Our first focus is New York and New Jersey. Once the experience is stable and verified in these areas, we plan to expand to other cities in the United States, in shaa’ Allah.
How do you make sure places are really halal?
We combine public information, input from owners and feedback from local Muslims. Official certifications will be clearly marked when available, and we will always welcome community correction if something is wrong.
Can my business advertise on Halal Map Prime?
Yes, in the future we plan to offer promotion options inside the app and on this website, especially during Ramadan and weekends. For early partnership discussions, email us at halalmapprime@outlook.com.
