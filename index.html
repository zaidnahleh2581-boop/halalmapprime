Why Halal Map Prime?

Designed for Muslims who live, work or travel in New York & New Jersey and want to quickly find trusted halal spots and places to pray.

🗺️
Map-first exploration

Browse halal places visually on a modern map instead of scrolling endless generic lists. Restaurants, mosques, markets & more.

✅
Verified halal listings

Focus on halal-friendly locations only. We highlight Muslim-owned or clearly halal-certified places as the app grows.

🕌
Mosques & prayer spaces

Find nearby mosques and masallah quickly, perfect for daily prayers, Jumu’ah or Taraweeh in Ramadan.

🛒
Markets & butchers

Discover halal meat shops and ethnic markets so you can cook halal at home with peace of mind.

🌙
Ramadan & Hijri tools (soon)

Ramadan calendar, Imsak & Iftar times, Hijri dates and helpful reminders – all inside the same app, in shaa Allah.

📍
Built in New York

Created by a member of the local community, focused first on NY & NJ – then expanding to more cities as we grow.

A clean, modern experience

Simple enough for anyone to use, powerful enough to cover your daily halal needs – food, prayer & community.

  • ✓ Search by area, category or name to find halal restaurants & mosques near you.

  • ✓ Use our posters & QR codes in mosques, markets and restaurants to quickly open the app.
  • ✓ Stay updated when we add new areas, features and Ramadan tools.
