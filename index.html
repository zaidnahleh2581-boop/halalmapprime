What you’ll find inside the app

Halal Map Prime is built to make everyday life easier for Muslims and halal-conscious families living in New York & New Jersey.

🍽️
Halal Restaurants
Discover nearby halal restaurants, food trucks and cafés with ratings, basic info and halal confidence notes.
🕌
Mosques & Prayer Spaces
Locate masajid and prayer spaces so you never miss a jama’ah when you’re out in the city.
🛒
Markets & Butchers
Find halal markets and butchers for groceries, meat and everyday essentials you can trust.
🕰️
Prayer Times & Ramadan Tools
Upcoming features include local prayer times, a Hijri calendar and Ramadan schedule for NY & NJ.
⭐
Community Feedback
Ratings, reviews and community signals to highlight clean, family-friendly and trusted locations.
📍
Built for NY & NJ
The app focuses specifically on New York and New Jersey to offer a deep, accurate halal map for the local community.

Future features

We are exploring additional features that serve the spiritual side of daily life:

  • Local prayer time calculations for NY & NJ cities.
  • Simple Hijri calendar and Ramadan schedule.
  • Basic daily adhkar and wudu guide for new Muslims and youth.
  • Optional notifications and gentle reminders for key prayer times.

Our intention is to keep everything respectful, simple and in line with local app store rules and community standards.
