App features

What you'll find inside the app

Map-first experience for halal life in NY & NJ

The app is built around a clean, fast map view – so you can move across boroughs, highways and neighborhoods and instantly see halal options around you.

Verified halal places Each listing is clearly marked (halal-certified, owner-verified or community-verified).
Smart filters Filter by restaurants, mosques, markets, butchers – and more categories as we grow.
Arabic & English UI The app launches in English by default with optional Arabic (and more languages later).
Prayer & calendar tools Daily prayer times and a full monthly timetable, right inside the app and on this site.
