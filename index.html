What Halal Map Prime offers

A simple, modern experience that connects halal lifestyle needs in one place.

🍽️
Halal Restaurants

Discover nearby halal spots – Arab, Turkish, Desi, African and more – with clear categories, ratings and delivery info.

🕌
Mosques & Prayer Spaces

Find masajid and prayer locations quickly, see approximate distance, and navigate via Google Maps.

🥩
Halal Butchers & Markets

Locate halal meat shops and Islamic markets that your family can trust for daily groceries.

🕒
Prayer & Spiritual Tools

Hijri date, simple prayer time view for NY/NJ, daily adhkar and reminders – all in one app experience (coming with the iOS app).

Prayer focus & community
Halal Map Prime is not just food – it’s built around salah, masajid and community well-being.
  • ✔ Simple prayer-time view (NY/NJ – app feature)
  • ✔ Hijri date & Ramadan calendar (in-app)
  • ✔ Short daily adhkar and reminders
  • ✔ Future plans: Qibla, notifications, and local events

All spiritual tools will live inside the iOS app experience. The website is your official hub for info, partners and updates.
