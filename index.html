What you will find inside Halal Map Prime

The app experience is focused on clarity, trust and simplicity – especially for families and everyday use.

🍽️
Halal Restaurants

Discover nearby halal spots – shawarma, biryani, burgers, mandi, gyros and more – with clear tags, ratings and delivery info.

🕌
Mosques & Prayer Spots

Quickly find masajid and prayer rooms, then open directions in Google Maps with one tap.

🥩
Butchers & Islamic Markets

Locate halal butchers and markets that serve the local Muslim community in NY & NJ.

🕒
Prayer & Spiritual Tools

Hijri dates, simple prayer-time view, Ramadan calendar, basic adhkar and reminders – all planned as core app features.

Why partner with Halal Map Prime?

Our goal is to make it easier for Muslims in NY & NJ to find halal places they can trust.

  • Appear inside the app when users search for halal food or mosques.
  • Be included on the website and social campaigns as a trusted partner.
  • Benefit from QR posters placed in masajid, markets and events.
  • Reach people who actively search for halal options every day.
