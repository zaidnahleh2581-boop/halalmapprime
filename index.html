FOR RESTAURANTS · MOSQUES · MARKETS · BUTCHERS

Partner with Halal Map Prime

If you run a halal restaurant, café, food truck, butcher, market or mosque in NY or NJ, you can join our map and reach local Muslims, families, students and travellers.

How it works

  • 1. Contact us by email and share your basic info (name, address, halal type, website, phone).
  • 2. We verify your details and add your spot to our internal list.
  • 3. When the app launches, your business will appear on the map with clear halal tags.
  • 4. Later, we may offer simple featured placement & promotions for partners who want extra visibility.
✅ For now: listing & verification are completely free while we prepare the launch.

Who we’re building this for

Muslim families, students, workers, delivery drivers and travellers in New York & New Jersey who need:

  • • Trusted halal food options (not just “maybe halal”).
  • • Clear prayer spaces and masjids near their work or university.
  • • Local halal butchers & markets for home cooking.

If you’d like to support the project as a sponsor or advertising partner, please mention “Business / Sponsor” in your email subject.
