Core features

What Halal Map Prime is built to do

A focused tool to help Muslims in New York & New Jersey find halal options quickly – without the noise, confusion or guesswork.

Halal map for NY & NJ

Explore restaurants, mosques, markets and halal butchers across New York and New Jersey, with a clean dark map inside the app and smart filters.

Prayer & Ramadan tools

Inside the app: prayer times, a simple Hijri / Ramadan calendar, reminders for Fajr & Maghrib, and helpful basics like wudu steps and daily adhkar.

Community-friendly design

No random content, no distractions. Just focused features for Muslim families, students and workers in the NY/NJ area.

FAQ

Questions about Halal Map Prime

Here are a few quick answers. More details will be available on the App Store page once the app is live, in shaa’ Allah.

Which areas does the app focus on?

The first version focuses on New York City and New Jersey: halal restaurants, mosques, markets and halal butchers in areas with active Muslim communities.

Is everything inside the app halal-verified?

We aim to prioritize clearly halal places and community-trusted locations. Over time, we plan to add clearer labels, certifications and owner-submitted details with manual review.

Will there be ads or paid plans?

For users, the app experience aims to stay clean and focused. For businesses, we may offer halal-friendly promotional options (like premium listings) that respect the community and local laws.

Does it support Android?

The first public launch targets iPhone (Apple App Store). Depending on community demand and resources, an Android version can follow in a later phase in shaa’ Allah.
